Safety Tips

AI is powerful, but smart habits keep you safe. Here’s a bigger list of practical steps you can follow to protect your information and your peace of mind:

  • Use Strong Passwords: Create unique passwords for each account. Consider a password manager to keep them secure.
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication: Add an extra layer of security by requiring a code or app confirmation when logging in.
  • Check App Permissions: Review what your apps can access — like your camera, microphone, or contacts — and limit unnecessary permissions.
  • Mute Smart Speakers: Turn off the mic when not using voice assistants to prevent accidental recordings.
  • Update Regularly: Always install the latest updates for your devices to patch security vulnerabilities.
  • Verify Sources: Don’t trust everything an AI chatbot or automated reply says. Cross-check health and finance advice with a human expert.
  • Be Scam Aware: Watch out for phishing emails crafted with AI that look very real. Double-check links and email addresses.
  • Educate Family: Talk with kids and older family members about AI, scams, and privacy basics to keep everyone safe.

Being mindful doesn’t mean avoiding AI — it means using it on your terms. Stay alert and share these tips with people you care about.
